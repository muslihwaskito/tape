T-Ape is a virtual reality Mataverse game that is played from a first-person perspective using the Virtual reality method. Flu-RX has wiped out half of the human population and has resulted in the rebellion of highly intelligent apes. Role-player of an ape imprisoned in a research facility by humans looking for a cure. The player's goal is to defend himself and rejoin Avi's group of monkeys. Along the way, players team up with two other captured apes, Ytre and Rtai. T-Ape is a game played on a linear path, with the player performing ape-like movements to advance to a predetermined place in the environment. Human guards throughout the game and must be killed before continuing. Players can use three types of weapons, and can take cover during firefights. Players can also climb pipes and swing from the ceiling Welcome to our Revolution !
